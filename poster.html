Introduction
  • Students lose focus and forget critical lecture content due to distractions and cognitive overload
  • Traditional note-taking is slow, incomplete, and prone to error during fast-paced lectures
  • No seamless tool exists to capture, process, and recall academic content in real time
  • The gap between hearing and retaining information is the core challenge in modern education
🎯
Objective
  • Improve attention through real-time audio monitoring and smart alerts
  • Enhance memory retention with AI-generated summaries and structured notes
  • Enable quick, intelligent recall of past lectures via a personal knowledge base
  • Create a seamless, wearable learning companion that works passively
⚠️
Problem Statement
  • Loss of focus — distractions break learning continuity during lectures
  • Missed information — critical concepts are lost when attention lapses
  • Poor revision methods — manual re-reading is inefficient and time-consuming
  • No smart indexing — students cannot search or query past lecture content easily
💡
Proposed Solution
  • BLUEMING captures live lecture audio passively through smart Bluetooth earpods
  • AI converts speech to text using advanced speech recognition models
  • NLP summarization extracts key concepts and condenses content automatically
  • Summaries are securely stored in the cloud and accessible via a mobile app anytime
